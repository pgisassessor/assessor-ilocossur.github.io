Quarterly Meeting of the Municipal and Provincial Assessors of Ilocos Sur

Conducted last May 30, 2024, Thursday at exaclty 9:00am, the Ilocos Sur Assessors League, Inc. together with the Provincial Assessor held their quarterly meeting at the Provincial Farmers Livelihood Development Center, Vigan City. The agenda of this meeting is to elect new set of officers whereas new President and Vice President of the league was appointed while the other retained their positions. Assessors tackled also some updates on Real Property Tax Mapping System (RPTAMS) and updates on assessment and appraisal activities. As the meeting advances, Municipal Assessors raised their concerns and problems encountered during the operation or dry run of their RPTAMS in their respective municipalities. The meeting was proceeding well and actions were taken to addressed all concerns.
